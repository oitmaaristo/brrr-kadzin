# brrr.kadzin Brand Guidelines

## Logo

**Primary:** `brrr.kadzin`
- Font: Outfit 600
- The dot is part of the name (like a domain)
- Dot color: Teal (#00d4aa)
- Lowercase always

**Short:** `bk`

## Colors

| Name | Hex | Usage |
|------|-----|-------|
| Deep Navy | `#1a1a2e` | Primary background |
| Teal | `#00d4aa` | Accent, the dot, success states |
| Electric Blue | `#4361ee` | Secondary accent, links |
| Light Gray | `#e0e0e0` | Body text on dark |
| Pure White | `#ffffff` | Headers, emphasis |

## Typography

- **Headers:** Outfit 600
- **Body:** Inter 400
- **Code:** JetBrains Mono

## Tone of Voice

- Technical but accessible
- Confident, not arrogant
- Direct, no fluff
- Occasional humor (BRRR!)
- Data-driven claims only

## Taglines

- "Make the printer go BRRR"
- "Where Smart Money meets AI"
- "SMC + AI = BRRR"
- "Pass your prop firm challenge. First try."

## Don'ts

- Don't use all caps (except BRRR)
- Don't use emojis in product UI
- Don't make unverified profit claims
- Don't claim official ICT endorsement
